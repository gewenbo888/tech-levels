Technology Levels

技术等级 L1–L5 — 从基础到文明飞跃

A unified maturity framework mapping 11 technological domains from foundational capability (L1) to civilizational transcendence (L5). Each level represents an order-of-magnitude leap.

一个统一的成熟度框架，将11个技术领域从基础能力（L1）映射到文明跨越（L5）。每个等级代表一个数量级的飞跃。
